About Us

Welcome to the Chino Valley Chamber of Commerce Foundation, a beacon of support and development in the heart of our vibrant community. As a cornerstone for progress and economic vitality, our Foundation is dedicated to nurturing the economic interests and welfare of the Chino Valley region. Through our unwavering commitment to community good projects, we aim to create a flourishing environment where businesses and community life can thrive.

"To empower and elevate the Chino Valley by supporting projects that enrich the community and drive economic growth."

Our mission is straightforward yet profound: to support and advance the economic interests of the Chino Valley region. We believe in the power of community, and our initiatives are designed to foster a strong, interconnected economic ecosystem. From supporting local businesses to funding community improvement projects, every action we take aims to enhance the quality of life for every resident and ensure our region's prosperity.
